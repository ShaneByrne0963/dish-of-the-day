Chocolate Chip Cookies

There's nothing better than homemade cookies! If done properly, they'll turn out nice and soft on the inside, and once you master this recipe, you'll never go back to buying cookies in the shop again. This recipe makes a lot of cookies, so you'll be able to share them with all your friends and family

Ingredients:

  • 227g Salted Butter, softened
  • 240g Granulated Sugar
  • 240g Light Brown Sugar
  • 2tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 720g Plain Flour
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1tsp Bread Soda
  • 1/2tsp Baking Powder
  • 1tsp Salt
  • 500g Chocolate Chips/Chunks

Method:

  1. Preheat your oven to 190°C (375°F). Line a baking tray with parchment paper and set aside
  2. In a separate bowl mix flour, bread soda, baking powder and salt. Set aside
  3. Cream butter and sugars together until combined
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla until fluffy
  5. Mix in the dry ingredients until combined
  6. Add the chocolate chips and mix until fully combined
  7. Roll individually into balls of your preferred size (We'd reccommend a 3cm diameter). Place them evenly spaced onto the lined tray
  8. Bake for 8-10 minutes. Take them out of the oven when they are starting to turn brown. If they don't look done, that's normal
  9. Leave on the tray for approximately 2 minutes before moving each of them to a cooling rack
  10. Repeat steps 7-9 with the rest of the cookie mix

Enjoy!
