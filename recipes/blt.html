B.L.T.

Short for Bacon, Lettuce and Tomato, this spin on the classic sandwich includes a sweet honey and mustard dressing that elegantly compliments the crispy bacon.

Ingredients:

  • 4 Slices Smoked Streaky Bacon
  • 1 Tomato, Sliced
  • 1-2 Leaves Romaine Lettuce
  • 2 Slices of Bread of Your Choice
  • 3tbsp Mayonnaise
  • 1/2tsp Honey
  • 1/2tsp Whole Grain Mustard

Method:

  1. Lay the streaky bacon on a cooking tray. Put in preheated oven set to 200°C (392°F)
  2. Mix mayonnaise, honey and mustard in a bowl.
  3. Slice the tomato, and break apart and wash the lettuce.
  4. When the bacon is getting close to the desired level of cooked, toast the bread.
  5. When everything is ready, assemble the sandwich as you like.

Enjoy!
