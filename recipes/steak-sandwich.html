Steak Sandwich

Served on top of crispy garlic bread and topped with a soft cheddar sauce, this recipe takes a bit more time than most of the others, but it's definitely worth it. If you have time and want to really treat yourself, then this is the choice for you!

Ingredients:

For Garlic Bread:

  • 2 Slices of Bread of Your Choice
  • 100g Butter
  • 1 Clove Garlic
  • Pinch of Parsley

For Steak Mix:

  • 400g Steak, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 Onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 Chilli Pepper, sliced
  • 1 Bell Pepper, thinly sliced
  • Pinch of Cumin
  • Pinch of Chilli Powder
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 2tbsp Olive Oil

For Cheddar Sauce:

  • 10g Butter
  • 10g Plain Flour
  • 150ml Milk
  • Pinch of Chilli Powder
  • 200g Grated Cheddar

Method:

  1. For the garlic butter, peel clove of garlic and put in a food processor and blitz
  2. Add the butter and parsley, then blitz again. Spread on the slices of bread and set aside for later
  3. Next, mix all the ingredients for the steak mix in a mixing bowl and set aside
  4. Heat your oven to 200 C and place a frying pan set to high heat and a saucepan set to medium heat on your cooking stove
  5. Put the garlic bread in the oven and fry the steak mix in the frying pan
  6. For the cheddar sauce, melt the butter in the saucepan, then add the flour and stir continuously until the mix begins to brown
  7. Add the milk and chilli powder. Stir often, while also occasionally stirring the steak mix
  8. When the steak mix is fully cooked and soft. remove from the heat and leave aside until sauce is ready. Do the same with the garlic bread
  9. Add the grated cheddar to the sauce when it turns thick and smooth. Mix until fully combined
  10. Put the steak mix on top of the garlic bread and drizzle the cheddar sauce on top. This is an open sandwich, so you don't have to stack the two slices on top of each other

Enjoy!
