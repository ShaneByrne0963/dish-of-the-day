Tuna Melt

If you love sandwiches and also love fish, then this is the recipe for you!

-Requested by Arthur Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tin Tuna
  • 2tbsp Mayonnaise
  • Juice of 1/4 Lime
  • 1/2 Stalk Spring Onion
  • Grated Cheese of Your Choice
  • Bread of Your Choice

Method:

  1. Thoroughly drain water from tuna. Mix with mayonnaise and lime juice
  2. Thinly slice spring onion. Mix with tuna mayo
  3. Put it all on a slice of bread. Top with cheese and cover with the other slice
  4. Toast in an oven or a toastie grill

Enjoy!
