Pasta Bolognese

Bolognese is one of the most recognisable pasta dishes out there, so the pressure is on us to make this one right. But don't worry, we think we got this one covered! Serves 4 people

Ingredients:

  • 600g Mince
  • 1 Onion, Finely Diced
  • 5 Cloves Garlic, Finely Diced
  • 50ml Milk
  • 50ml Water
  • 1 Tin (400g) Chopped Tomatoes
  • 3 tbsp Tomato Puree
  • Pinch of Oregano
  • Pinch of Parsley
  • Pinch of Basil
  • Pinch of Spaghetti
  • 320g Pasta
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese (For Top)

Method:

  1. Fry the mince, onion and garlic in a large, hot, oiled pot, stirring often. Simmer until all the juices released by it are evaporated
  2. Add the milk, and continue to simmer until the water is reduced again. This will tenderise the meat and make it softer
  3. Add all other ingredients. Cook for 1 hour or until the meat is tender
  4. When the sauce is beginning to turn thick, begin to boil the pasta in boiling salted water
  5. The bolognese sauce should be cooked by the time the pasta cooks. Strain the pasta and serve by either mixing with the sauce or pouring the sauce on top. Top with parmesan if you like

Enjoy!
