Pancakes

Wether you like them with sweet berries or crispy bacon, this buttermilk pancake recipe will be the perfect start to your day!

Ingredients:

  • 150g Plain Flour
  • 2tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/4tsp Bread Soda
  • 50g Caster Sugar
  • 150ml Buttermilk
  • 25g Melted Butter
  • 2 Eggs

Method:

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, bread soda and sugar in a mixing bowl. Form a well (hole) in the center of the mixture.
  2. Mix all the other ingredients in a separate container.
  3. Pour the wet mix slowly into the well of the dry mix, stirring constantly.
  4. To cook the pancakes, pour the desired amount of mixture for one pancake into a hot, greased pan. Flip the pancake after one minute, or when golden brown on the bottom. Repeat with the other side, then remove from the pan and serve.

Enjoy!
