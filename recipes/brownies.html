Chocolate Brownies

Everybody loves brownies, so that's why we thought we had to have a recipe for one here. This recipe includes walnuts to give it an extra layer of flavour!

Ingredients:

  • 125g Plain Chocolate
  • 200g Butter
  • 2 Eggs
  • 200g Caster Sugar
  • 100g Plain Flour
  • 125g Walnuts, roughly chopped

Method:

  1. Line a baking tray with parchment paper. Preheat the oven to 180°C (356°F)
  2. Melt the butter and chocolate in a saucepan over a low heat, stirring until melted. Set aside to cool
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs and sugar until thick and foamy
  4. Whisk in the chocolate butter mix. Then fold in the flour and walnuts
  5. Pour into the lined tray and bake for 20-25 minutes
  6. To check if it's finished, run a knife through the brownie. If the knife comes out clean, they are done

Enjoy!
