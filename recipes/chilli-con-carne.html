Chilli Con Carne

One of my personal favourites, this chilli can be served in all kinds of ways: with rice, fries or with tortilla chips for a party. Whatever you decide, it's gonna taste good! Serves 4 people

Ingredients:

  • 600g Mince
  • 50ml Milk
  • 1 Tin (400g) Chopped Tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp Tomato Puree
  • 100g Sweetcorn
  • 100g Black Beans/Kidney Beans
  • 2 Chillies, Sliced
  • Pinch of Cumin
  • Pinch of Chilli Powder
  • Pinch of Smoked Paprika
  • Pinch of Cayenne
  • Base of Your Choice (Rice, French Fries or Tortilla Chips)

Method:

  1. Fry the mince in a large, hot, oiled pot, stirring constantly. Simmer until all the juices released by it are evaporated.
  2. Add the milk, and continue to simmer until the water is reduced again. This will tenderise the meat and make it softer.
  3. Add all other ingredients. cook for 1 hour or until the meat is tender.
  4. Serve with base of your choice

Enjoy!
