Vegetable Soup

You can't go wrong with vegetable soup! Packed full of nutrients and incredibly easy to make, you can have a pot going in no time and have enough to last you for days

-Requested by James O'Shea

Ingredients:

  • 5 Medium Rooster Potatoes
  • 2 White Onions
  • 2 Sticks Celery
  • 1 Leek
  • 2 Carrots
  • 1 Parsnip
  • 3-4 Vegetable Stock Cubes
  • A hand blender

Method:

  1. Peel the potatoes and dice into small chunks. Put in a large pot and cover with water to stop them from browning
  2. Remove the outer skin of the onions and cut in any shape. Because we are going to blend the soup in the end the size doesn't matter but the smaller the veg is cut, the faster it will cook
  3. Cut the top (the dark leafy part) and bottom part off the leek, slice vertically in half and run under a tap, washing every layer if the celery to remove any dirt inside. Then slice
  4. Cut the tips and the white bottom of the celery sticks and slice
  5. Peel the carrots and parsnip, remove the tops and bottoms and slice
  6. Put all the prepared veg into the pot with the potatoes. Fill with water to just cover all the veg
  7. Put the stock cubes into the pot and boil until the veg is soft
  8. Use the hand blender to blend until smooth. If the soup is very thick, add more water
  9. Add more stock cubes/salt if necessary

Enjoy!
