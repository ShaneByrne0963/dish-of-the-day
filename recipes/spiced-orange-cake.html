Spiced Orange Cake

This cake is perfect for the holiday season, because oranges are in season, but can be enjoyed all year round. Not only is this orange cake irresistable, but it's also gluten free!

Ingredients:

  • 2 Large Oranges
  • 300g Ground Almonds
  • 300g Caster Sugar
  • 2tsp Baking Powder
  • 1tsp Cinnamon
  • 1tsp Ginger
  • 7 Eggs

Method:

  1. Boil the oranges for 1.5 to 2 hours. Blitz and keep 450g of the pulp in a food processor. Discard the rest
  2. Place the pulp back in the processor with all of the dry ingredients and blitz
  3. While blitzing, add 1 egg at a time until the batter is smooth
  4. Pour the batter into a lined cake tin and bake at 170°C (330°F)
  5. After 30 minutes, take out of the oven and cover with tin foil. Then place back in the oven for another 20-30 minutes
  6. To check if it's finished, run a knife down the center of the cake. If the knife comes out clean, the cake is done

Enjoy!
