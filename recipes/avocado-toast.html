Avocado Toast

A super simple yet healthy breakfast, this choice is ideal for the ones who don't have a lot of time in the mornings but still want to eat healthy. With enough practice, you'll be able to make the avocado before your bread is even toasted!

Ingredients:

  • 2 Slices of Bread of Your Choice
  • 1 Avocado
  • Juice of 1/2 Lime
  • Pinch of Chilli Flakes (Optional)
  • 1 Stalk Spring Onion, sliced (Optional)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Method:

  1. Toast your bread using your preferred method (toaster, oven, grill etc.)
  2. While the bread is toasting, cut the avocado in half, avoiding cutting through the seed in the middle
  3. Using a knife to remove the avocado seed can be dangerous, and is completely unnecessary. If it doesn't fall out on it's own, simply pop it out by pressing on the outer layer.
  4. Scoop out the inside of the avocado using a spoon. Then mix with the other ingredients
  5. When the bread is toasted to your liking, spread the avocado mixture on top and serve. It really is that simple!

Enjoy!
